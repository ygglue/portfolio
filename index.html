Pokémon Sapphire

Pokémon Sapphire is a role-playing game for the Game Boy Advance set in the tropical Hoenn region. Players take on the role of a Pokémon Trainer, capturing and training creatures to battle against other Trainers while thwarting the villainous Team Aqua’s plans to flood the world’s seas. With over 100 new Pokémon to discover, enhanced graphics, and the ability to link with Pokémon Ruby for trading and battling, it offers an adventurous journey filled with exploration, strategy, and friendship.

Pokémon Ruby

DPokémon Ruby is a role-playing adventure for the Game Boy Advance set in the diverse Hoenn region. Players journey as a Pokémon Trainer, capturing and raising Pokémon to challenge Gym Leaders and ultimately the Pokémon League, all while stopping the nefarious Team Magma from expanding the world’s landmass. Featuring over 100 new Pokémon, vibrant visuals, and the ability to connect with Pokémon Sapphire for trading and battling, it delivers a rich blend of exploration, strategy, and competition.
